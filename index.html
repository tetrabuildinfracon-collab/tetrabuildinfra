About Us

Georgia’s New-Generation Infrastructure Contractor

Tetrabuild Infracon LLC is a Georgia-based general contractor focused on roadway, utility, and civil infrastructure projects with a modern, technology-driven mindset.

Tetrabuild Infracon LLC is a new-generation general contractor based in Georgia, founded by professionals with hands-on experience delivering roadway, utility, and civil infrastructure projects across the state. We focus on GDOT work, municipal improvements, and private site developments—with a core commitment to safety, quality, and on-schedule performance.

As we introduce our company and new digital presence to the market, we bring a modern, technology-driven mindset to construction. Our strength comes from combining detailed project planning with practical field execution, ensuring that every project is built right the first time.

At Tetrabuild Infracon, we stand for reliable infrastructure, clear communication, and results that our clients can count on.

How We Work

We align preconstruction, field operations, and project controls from day one—leveraging CPM scheduling, cost tracking, and utility coordination to keep work predictable and transparent for owners, partners, and stakeholders.

Services & Expertise

Construction & Consulting Under One Roof

We deliver heavy civil construction in the field and project management support behind the scenes—helping clients plan, build, and close out projects with confidence.

Core Construction Services
  • Roadway Improvement & Widening
    Precision-built upgrades that enhance capacity, safety, and long-term performance.
  • Earthwork & Grading
    Mass excavation, site shaping, and fine grading completed with accuracy and strict quality control.
  • Storm Drain & Utility Installation
    Reliable drainage and underground utility systems constructed to GDOT and municipal standards.
  • Erosion & Sediment Control
    Proactive BMPs that keep projects compliant, safe, and efficient throughout construction.
  • Concrete Paving & Structures
    Sidewalks, curb & gutter, pads, pavement, and structural concrete built for durability.
  • Roadway Sign Installation
    Permanent signs, posts, and assemblies installed for safer, clearer transportation routes.
  • Advanced Warning Sign Installation
    Temporary traffic control and work-zone setups that protect both workers and the traveling public.
Professional & Consulting Services
  • Project Management Consulting
    Leadership and guidance for owners, contractors, and municipalities to keep work moving forward.
  • Utility Coordination
    Streamlined planning and communication with utility companies to avoid conflicts and delays.
  • Construction Claims & Analysis
    Delay evaluations, time impact analyses, and documentation that support fair, accurate claims.
  • Cost Control & Budget Analysis
    Data-driven reviews to help clients stay ahead of overruns and make informed financial decisions.
  • Project Scheduling & Management
    CPM schedule development, updates, and oversight to keep projects on track from notice-to-proceed to close-out.
